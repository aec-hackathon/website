Rebuild Los Angeles

June 20-22 2025

The AEC Hackathon Rebuild Los Angeles edition brings together built environment professionals, technologists, and others to develop innovative solutions and help those effected by the recent fires.

This special edition unites Los Angeles locals with the global AEC Hackathon community and construction experts from Europe to create cost-effective, sustainable, and resilient rebuilding strategies. Over one weekend, participants collaborate on real-world challenges to deliver impactful solutions that can be rapidly deployed.

By harnessing technology, open-source innovation, and community-driven problem-solving, this hackathon is a step toward rebuilding stronger and smarter. Join us in shaping the future of disaster recovery.
